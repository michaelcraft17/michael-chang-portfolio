Research Experience
Cal Poly Pomona · SoftCom Lab
Computer Vision & Autonomous Robotics
Summer 2024 – Present
  • Developed a real-time tennis ball detection system on Raspberry Pi 4 using a custom-trained TensorFlow Lite CNN model, achieving mAP approaching 0.99 across 100+ training epochs.
  • Curated and annotated a domain-specific image dataset under varied lighting and occlusion; diagnosed training instability through loss function analysis and hyperparameter tuning.
  • Engineered a multi-modal sensor pipeline — camera, ultrasonic sonar, infrared — for robust obstacle detection and path-planning on an autonomous mobile chassis.
  • Benchmarked against YOLO, Faster R-CNN, and SSD; documented trade-offs in speed, accuracy, and domain generalizability.
California Science & Technology University
SLAM & Autonomous Bipedal Soccer Robot
Summer 2026
  • Designed and implemented an autonomous bipedal soccer robot using a micro:bit V2 microcontroller and XIAO ESP32S3 Sense vision coprocessor.
  • Integrated a mounted camera on the RobotPu platform to detect soccer balls and goal posts, enabling autonomous navigation, alignment, and kicking.
  • Developed a full perception-planning-action pipeline with I2C sensor fusion, Kalman filtering, a viewpoint pose controller, and a finite state machine.
Harvard University
Smart Elder Care Monitoring Initiative
